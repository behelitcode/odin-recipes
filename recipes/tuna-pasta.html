Tuna Pasta

Description

An original recipe made after a lot of trial and error.

The Ingredients consist of a mix of items that don't sound appetizing at first.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Canned tuna
  • Seven spices mix
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lettuce and parsley
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Prepare pasta
  2. Chop parsley and lettuce
  3. Add canned tuna
  4. Mix in the other ingredients
