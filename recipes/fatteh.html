Fatteh

Description

Fatteh is a levantine dish famous for it's diverse mix of flavors and the different ways it's prepared based on each country.

A regular hummus based recipe is obviously made with boiled chickpeas and a type of sour yogurt called "laban".

Other toppings like cashews, pine nuts, parsley and dried pomegranate seeds are usually added on top with some fried pita bread to finish it off.

Ingredients

  • Yogurt
  • Boiled chickpeas
  • Fried pita bread
  • Fried mixed nuts
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Boil chickpeas
  2. Prepare yogurt
  3. Fry cashews and pine nuts
  4. Fry pita bread
  5. Chop parsley
  6. Assemble the Ingredients
