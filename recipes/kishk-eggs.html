Kishk With Eggs

Description

Kishk is a Lebanese ingredient prepared with dried bulgur and yogurt. It can be made into multiple dishes like soup and pies, but in my opinion adding it to scrambled eggs is one of the best recipes.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Kishk

Steps

  1. Put some butter in a pan
  2. Make scrambled eggs
  3. Add kishk powder
  4. Mix in water until you have your preferred consistency
